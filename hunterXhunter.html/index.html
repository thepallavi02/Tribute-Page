HUNTER X HUNTER

Action | Adventure | Fantasy | Drama

It is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Yoshihiro Togashi.The story focuses on a young boy named Gon Freecss, who discovers that his father Ging Freecss, who left him at a young age, is actually a world-renowned Hunter. Gon departs on a journey to become a Hunter and eventually find his father. Along the way, Gon meets various other Hunters and encounters the paranormal.
